Quiz Maker: Create a quiz to challenge your audience

Make fun interactive quizzes to test your colleagues’ knowledge, run a quiz night with friends, or help students study.

What can I use quizzes for at work?
You can use quizzes for team building, training, assessments, and more.
How many questions can I add to a quiz?
There is usually no limit, but it depends on the platform or tool you're using.
How do participants join and compete in my quiz?
Participants can join via a shared link or code, depending on the quiz platform.
Is there a limit to the number of participants?
Some platforms may have a limit on participants. Check your platform for specifics.
What types of questions can I include in the quiz?
You can include multiple-choice, true/false, short answer, and more types of questions.
What's the difference between quiz slides and question slides?
Quiz slides may contain instructions or information, while question slides contain the actual quiz questions.
